May 2026 · Physician of the Month
Dr. Priyanka Sanjay, M.D.
Board-Certified Internist  ·  Novamedcare Inc  ·  Piscataway, NJ
ABIM Certified · Internal Medicine Piscataway, NJ JWPA Member since 2018–2019

"I'm grateful for the opportunity to serve my community while having the unwavering support of my family. Their encouragement allows me to embrace the daily lessons in humility that medicine provides and fuels my growth as both a person and a physician."

— Dr. Priyanka Sanjay, M.D. · Internal Medicine

Dr. Priyanka Sanjay is a board-certified internist with over 20 years of experience, committed to guiding her patients toward better health and overall well-being. She practices at Novamedcare Inc, 216 Stelton Rd, Suite B3, Piscataway, NJ, proudly serving communities across Middlesex, Mercer, and Somerset counties.

Beyond her clinical work, Dr. Sanjay actively volunteers in community-focused initiatives throughout New Jersey, reflecting her deep commitment to giving back. A former state-level table tennis player with multiple awards, she was recognized as "Best Outgoing Sportsperson" in her final year of medical school — a testament to her drive and character both inside and outside of medicine. She enjoys spending time outdoors and traveling, finding that exploring different cultures and cuisines enriches her thoughtful, personalized approach to patient care.

How It Works

The nomination process

01
Submit a Nomination

Any JWPA member can nominate a fellow member. Email info@jwpa.org with the physician's name, specialty, institution, and why they deserve the spotlight.

02
Review by Leadership

JWPA leadership reviews nominations each month, considering clinical excellence, mentorship, community impact, and alignment with the JWPA mission.

03
Featured Across JWPA

The selected physician is featured on this page, in the JWPA newsletter, and across JWPA social channels — celebrating their contributions to medicine.
