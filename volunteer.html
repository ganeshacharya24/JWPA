How You Can Help

Volunteer opportunities

Flagship Program
TWS Physician Volunteer

Spend a day mentoring high school students in the Teens With Stethoscopes program. Lead a clinical station, sit on a panel, or simply share your journey into medicine. This is JWPA's most direct way to build the next generation of women physicians.

One day per year · Annual TWS event
Community · Members Only
Mentorship Circles — Mentor

When Mentorship Circles launches, experienced physicians will be matched with early-career members for ongoing guidance and peer support. Register your interest now so we can reach out when the program opens.

Ongoing · JWPA members only · In development
Events
Event Support

Help organize and staff JWPA events — from registration and logistics to day-of coordination and outreach. A great way to get involved without a long-term commitment.

Per event · Flexible
Leadership
Speaker or Panelist

Share your expertise at a JWPA event — a networking evening, an advocacy discussion, or an annual celebration. We welcome physicians at all stages who have something to say and a story to tell.

Per engagement · Flexible
Express Interest

What your time makes possible

Every TWS volunteer helps a high school student see medicine as a real possibility — often for the first time.

Your involvement adds to a growing community of women physicians across all 21 New Jersey counties.

100+
