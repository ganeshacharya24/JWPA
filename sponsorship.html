Why Sponsor JWPA

Reach the physicians shaping healthcare in New Jersey

A JWPA sponsorship puts your organization in front of a concentrated, high-trust community of women physicians — attending physicians across every specialty practicing throughout New Jersey.

Our members are decision-makers. They recommend practices, refer patients, influence purchasing decisions in their hospitals, and shape the culture of the institutions they work in. A sponsorship isn't an ad — it's a genuine alignment with a community that values what you stand for.

Beyond visibility, sponsoring JWPA means investing in a mission that matters: advancing gender equity in medicine and inspiring the next generation of women physicians through programs like Teens With Stethoscopes.
