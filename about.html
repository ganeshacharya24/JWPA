Our Story

Founded to lift women in medicine

Founded in 2008, the Jersey Women Physician Alliance was built on a simple belief: women in medicine are stronger together. What began as a small network of New Jersey physicians has grown into a statewide community of 600+ members spanning every specialty — from primary care to surgery, academic medicine to private practice.

JWPA supports women physicians at every stage of their career through mentorship, education, and advocacy. We create opportunities to connect across specialties, build leadership skills, and expand community impact across New Jersey.

Our flagship program, Teens With Stethoscopes (TWS), extends that mission to the next generation — giving high school students a window into the world of medicine through real physician mentorship, hands-on experiences, and career panels.

Our Values

What we stand for

Four principles guide everything JWPA does — from the programs we build to the community we create.

Community

A statewide network connecting women physicians across all specialties, career stages, and institutions.

Advocacy

Promoting equity in healthcare leadership and policy at every level — local, state, and national.

Education

Equipping women physicians at every career stage with real-world support, mentorship, and lifelong learning opportunities.

Leadership

Encouraging and equipping women to take on leadership roles that shape the future of healthcare.

Our Journey

Key milestones

From a small network of NJ physicians to a statewide nonprofit — here's how JWPA grew.

2008
JWPA Founded

Dr. Sudha Nahar establishes the Jersey Women Physician Alliance in November 2008, built on the belief that women in medicine are stronger together.

2018
First Annual Gala

JWPA hosts its inaugural Annual Celebration & Awards Dinner, bringing together women physicians from across New Jersey.

2025
600+ Member Milestone

JWPA surpasses 600 active women physician members spanning all specialties — from primary care and surgery to academic medicine and private practice across all 21 NJ counties.

2026
TWS Inaugural Event — March 21, 2026

JWPA launches and hosts the first-ever Teens With Stethoscopes event, 9:00 AM–3:00 PM at the RWJUH Auditorium, Jack & Sheryl Morris Cancer Center. A milestone day marking the birth of JWPA's flagship student immersion program.
