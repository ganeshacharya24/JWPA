Your Impact

Every gift makes a difference

Here's what donations to JWPA make possible — directly.

$25
Student Materials

Covers clinical station supplies and materials for one TWS student participant — everything they need for a full day of immersive learning.

$100
Full TWS Experience

Sponsors a single student's complete TWS experience — from materials and registration to the physician panel and mentorship session.

$250
Mentorship Programming

Funds one quarter of JWPA's physician mentorship programming — session resources, coordination, and community materials.

$500
Community Event

Covers the cost of a JWPA community outreach event — venue, logistics, and physician volunteer coordination across New Jersey.

$1,000
Annual Program Support

Provides sustained support for one of JWPA's programs for an entire year — keeping the lights on for the work that matters most.

Any
Every Dollar Counts

No minimum. Whatever you give goes directly to supporting women physicians and the students who will become the next generation of doctors in NJ.
