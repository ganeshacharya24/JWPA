About JWPA

Built by physicians,
for physicians

Founded in 2008, the Jersey Women Physician Alliance supports women physicians across New Jersey through mentorship, education, and advocacy. We create opportunities to connect across specialties, build leadership skills, and expand community impact statewide.

From newly practicing physicians to those with decades of experience, JWPA is a home for every stage of a woman physician's career.

Programs & Initiatives

What we do

JWPA focuses on mentorship, leadership development, advocacy, and community outreach to advance women in medicine across New Jersey.

Mentorship Circles

Connect women physicians across specialties and career stages through structured mentorship and peer community — exclusively for JWPA members.

Leadership Development

Leadership panels, workshops, and skill-building sessions that equip women physicians for leadership roles across hospital systems and academic medicine.

Advocacy

Promote equity and representation in healthcare through awareness campaigns, policy education, and community-centered engagement.

Featured Program · Teens With Stethoscopes

Inspiring the next generation of physicians

A one-day immersive experience introducing high school students to medicine through hands-on activities, panels with women physicians, and guided mentorship — designed to spark a lifelong passion for healthcare.
