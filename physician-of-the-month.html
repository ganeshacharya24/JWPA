How It Works

The nomination process

01
Submit a Nomination

Any JWPA member can nominate a fellow member. Email info@jwpa.org with the physician's name, specialty, institution, and why they deserve the spotlight.

02
Review by Leadership

JWPA leadership reviews nominations each month, considering clinical excellence, mentorship, community impact, and alignment with the JWPA mission.

03
Featured Across JWPA

The selected physician is featured on this page, in the JWPA newsletter, and across JWPA social channels — celebrating their contributions to medicine.
