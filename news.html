TWS 2026 Applications Are Now Open

High school students across New Jersey can now apply for the 2026 Teens With Stethoscopes program — a free, one-day immersive experience introducing students to medicine through clinical stations, physician panels, and mentorship. Space is limited and the program fills quickly. Students interested in a future in healthcare are encouraged to apply early.
