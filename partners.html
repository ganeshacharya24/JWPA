Get Involved

Become a partner

JWPA partners with organizations that share our commitment to advancing women in medicine — from hospital systems and academic institutions to specialty practices, nonprofits, and corporate supporters.

A partnership with JWPA connects your organization to 100+ active women physicians across New Jersey, provides visibility at events and programs, and demonstrates a meaningful investment in gender equity in healthcare.

Hospital & Health System

Co-host events, provide clinical programming space, and connect your physicians to the JWPA network.

Academic & Residency Program

Support JWPA programs including TWS, mentorship circles, and leadership initiatives through academic collaboration.

Corporate & Industry

Sponsor events and programs to gain visibility with NJ's leading women physicians and demonstrate your commitment to equity.

Nonprofit & Community Organization

Collaborate on outreach, pipeline programs, and advocacy initiatives that serve NJ communities.

Partnership Inquiry
