Leadership Team

JWPA is led by its founder and supported by a dedicated leadership team of women physicians and healthcare professionals committed to advancing the mission across New Jersey.

Founder & President

The woman behind JWPA

SN
Replace src with Dr. Nahar's photo
Founder & President · Est. 2008
Dr. Sudha Nahar, MD
Gastroenterologist  ·  Gyan Gastroenterology LLC  ·  New Brunswick, NJ

Dr. Nahar founded JWPA in November 2008 with a singular belief: women in medicine are stronger together. A board-certified gastroenterologist and Board Member of the Medical Executive Committee at Robert Wood Johnson University Hospital, she brings both clinical expertise and institutional leadership to every aspect of JWPA's work.
